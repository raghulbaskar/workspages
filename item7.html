GOAL

The name "Franco" is a French word related to France.As this website is for a Fashion Ecommerce for redefining fashion ,situated in France, we came with this name which rightly justifies the very existence of the company. Its a multipurpose e commerce website where consumers can keep track of the latest trends in the fashion industry and find all variety of accesories to define their styles and create trends.

STO

RY.

As the name reached seas and beyond,they felt the need to create a website,so we came to their rescue.All franco needed was a website which is as trendy as its products and satisfied market needs.So we came up with a responsive design,supportive backend and a simple UX that can engage customers to deliver its reach worldwide.

Now that they have a website that is customised according to their service,goods and trends, we are just waiting for the establishment of Franco as an industry leader to reach and influence the masses.

PHOTOGRAPHY

We worked with Franco to create realistic photos that truly reflects styles and trends of industry. Photos are the result of hardwork of models and photographers who have helped to bridge the gap between online store and actual stores with the contents originality.
